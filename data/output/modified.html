Governance is a concept\nencompassing all aspects of the exercise of authority through formal and\ninformal institutions in the management of the resources of a state.  The\nquality of governance is thus determined by the impact of this exercise of\npower on the quality of life enjoyed by citizens.  There is a growing\nawareness in the community that an enabling environment is needed to impact on\nthe well-being of its citizens, such enabling environment are created by\ninterest, institutions and policies. Although no single index can conceptually\ncapture all aspects of this enabling environment, a focus on key observable\naspects of the governance dimensions can be helpful. The key observable aspects\nof the governance dimension considered are citizen’s voice, government\norientation, social development, and economic management. The ability to create\nan index of governance quality has been enhanced by the creation of several\nindexes in recent years.  For example, we make use of previously\npublished indexes measuring health provision, education, quality political\nfreedom, and government corruption.
